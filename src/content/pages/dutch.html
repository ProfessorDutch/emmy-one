Dutch

Jason "Dutch" Brown is an industry outsider who has redefined three American industries from the outside. He changed how the real estate and mortgage industries gather, qualify, and deliver buyers — systems behind millions of home loan transactions. He founded Modern Legal Finance, carrying legal fee financing bar by bar for fifteen years until the American Bar Association formally recognized the category in Formal Opinion 484. He is the creator of the Emergency Whitelist, the 6 AM moment, Prominence Theory, Crisis Response Management, and the Crisis to Whitelist Framework — and the founder of MeetEmmy, which brought crisis response management to the home service industry.

MeetEmmy is the third industry. It is the last stage of a longer record.

The record lives under his own name.
